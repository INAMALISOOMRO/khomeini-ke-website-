Comprehensive Diagnostic Testing

Our fully equipped laboratory provides a complete range of diagnostic testing services to support accurate medical diagnosis and treatment planning. We utilize cutting-edge technology and follow international quality standards to ensure reliable and timely results for all our patients.

With a team of highly qualified pathologists, microbiologists, and laboratory technicians, we process thousands of tests daily while maintaining the highest accuracy standards. Our lab is equipped with automated analyzers and modern equipment that deliver fast, precise results.

Laboratory Tests We Offer

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC) and Blood Chemistry
  • Liver Function Tests (LFT) and Kidney Function Tests (KFT)
  • Lipid Profile and Glucose Monitoring
  • Thyroid Function Tests (T3, T4, TSH)
  • Hormone Analysis and Fertility Tests
  • Urine Analysis and Stool Examination
  • Blood Grouping and Cross-Matching
  • Microbiology and Culture Tests
  • Serology and Immunology Tests
  • Tumor Markers and Cancer Screening
  • Cardiac Markers (Troponin, CK-MB)
  • COVID-19 PCR and Antigen Testing

Advanced Technology & Equipment

Our laboratory is equipped with fully automated analyzers from leading manufacturers, ensuring consistent accuracy and rapid turnaround times. We use digital systems for result management, allowing doctors to access reports quickly and securely.

Quality Assurance

We participate in external quality assurance programs and maintain rigorous internal quality control protocols. Regular calibration of equipment, proficiency testing, and staff training ensure that our laboratory maintains the highest standards of diagnostic excellence.

Our commitment to accuracy means we follow standardized procedures for sample collection, processing, and analysis. Every test goes through multiple quality checkpoints before results are released.

Our Laboratory Facilities

Sample Collection & Results

We offer convenient sample collection services with trained phlebotomists who ensure patient comfort during the process. For routine tests, results are typically available within 24 hours, while urgent cases can be processed on a priority basis.

Results can be collected in person or accessed digitally through our secure patient portal. Our staff is available to answer any questions about test results and can coordinate with your physician for proper interpretation.
