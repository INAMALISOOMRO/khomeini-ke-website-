Comprehensive Women's Healthcare

Our Maternity and Gynecology department provides complete healthcare services for women at every stage of life. From prenatal care to delivery, and gynecological health concerns, we offer compassionate, expert care in a comfortable, supportive environment.

Our team of experienced obstetricians and gynecologists, along with skilled midwives and nurses, work together to ensure the health and wellbeing of mothers and babies. We combine medical expertise with personalized attention to make your experience as comfortable as possible.

Prenatal Care

Regular prenatal visits are essential for a healthy pregnancy. Our comprehensive prenatal care includes physical examinations, ultrasound scans, blood tests, and health counseling. We monitor both mother and baby's health throughout pregnancy, addressing any concerns promptly.

Labor & Delivery

Our modern labor and delivery suites are designed to provide a comfortable, safe environment for childbirth. We support natural childbirth while being prepared for any complications. Our team is trained in both normal deliveries and emergency obstetric procedures.

We encourage family participation in the birthing process and provide options for pain management during labor. Our neonatal team is always on standby to ensure immediate care for newborns if needed.

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

For pregnancies with complications such as gestational diabetes, hypertension, multiple pregnancies, or previous cesarean sections, we provide specialized high-risk pregnancy care. Our team monitors these pregnancies closely and coordinates with other specialists when needed.

Postnatal Care

After delivery, we provide comprehensive postnatal care for both mother and baby. This includes monitoring recovery, wound care for cesarean sections, breastfeeding support, newborn care instructions, and emotional support during the adjustment period.

Our lactation consultants help new mothers with breastfeeding techniques and address any difficulties. We also provide guidance on newborn care, immunizations, and postpartum health.

Gynecological Health

We provide complete gynecological care including routine checkups, management of menstrual disorders, fertility issues, and menopausal symptoms. Our approach combines medical treatment with patient education and counseling.

Regular gynecological examinations are important for early detection of health issues. We offer cervical cancer screening, breast examinations, and other preventive health services for women.

Family Planning

We provide confidential family planning services and counseling on various contraceptive methods. Our doctors help you choose the most appropriate option based on your health, lifestyle, and family planning goals.

Women's Health Education

We believe in empowering women with knowledge about their health. Our team provides education on reproductive health, pregnancy care, nutrition during pregnancy, safe motherhood practices, and general women's health concerns.
