About Darul Shifa Imam Khomeini Hospital

Serving Humanity with Compassion and Excellence Since Our Foundation

Our Mission

Darul Shifa Imam Khomeini Hospital is a charitable healthcare institution dedicated to providing high-quality medical care to the people of Quetta and surrounding areas, regardless of their financial circumstances. We believe that healthcare is a fundamental human right, not a privilege.

Our mission is to serve humanity with compassion, dignity, and excellence. We are committed to making quality healthcare accessible to everyone, especially those who cannot afford expensive medical treatments.

Who We Are

A Charity Hospital for All

Darul Shifa Imam Khomeini Hospital operates as a non-profit charity hospital, dedicated to serving the community of Balochistan. We provide free and subsidized medical care to those in need, ensuring that no one is turned away due to lack of funds.

Our hospital is supported by generous donations and the tireless efforts of our medical staff who work with dedication and compassion to heal and care for every patient who walks through our doors.
