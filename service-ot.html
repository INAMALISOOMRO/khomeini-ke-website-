World-Class Surgical Care

At Darul Shifa Imam Khomeini Hospital, our Operation Theatre (OT) is designed to provide the highest standards of surgical care. We have invested in cutting-edge medical technology and equipment to ensure that every surgical procedure is performed with precision, safety, and the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our OT complex consists of multiple fully-equipped operation rooms, each maintained under strict sterile conditions. Every detail has been carefully considered to create an optimal setting for surgical excellence.

Our Surgical Capabilities

  • General Surgery - Appendectomy, hernia repairs, gallbladder surgery, and more
  • Orthopedic Surgery - Joint replacements, fracture fixations, spine surgery
  • Gynecological Surgery - C-sections, hysterectomy, and other procedures
  • Urological Surgery - Kidney stone removal, prostate surgery
  • ENT Surgery - Tonsillectomy, sinus surgery, ear procedures
  • Emergency Surgery - 24/7 trauma and emergency surgical services
  • Pediatric Surgery - Specialized care for young patients

Advanced Equipment & Technology

Our operation theatres are equipped with the latest surgical instruments and monitoring systems. We utilize advanced anesthesia equipment with continuous patient monitoring, ensuring maximum safety throughout every procedure. High-definition surgical lights, modular OT tables, and modern cautery units are standard in all our operating rooms.

Pre & Post-Operative Care

Our commitment to patient care extends beyond the operating room. We provide comprehensive pre-operative assessments to ensure patients are fully prepared for surgery, and our dedicated post-operative recovery rooms are staffed with skilled nurses who monitor patients closely as they recover from anesthesia.

We maintain detailed protocols for infection prevention, pain management, and patient comfort. Our surgical team works closely with anesthesiologists, nurses, and support staff to create a seamless surgical experience from admission to discharge.

Safety & Quality Standards

Patient safety is our top priority. All our surgical procedures follow international protocols and guidelines. We maintain strict sterilization procedures, conduct regular equipment maintenance, and ensure our staff receives ongoing training in the latest surgical techniques and safety practices.

Our infection control measures are comprehensive, including regular air quality monitoring, surface sterilization, and proper disposal of medical waste. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of surgical care.
