24/7 Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department operates around the clock, every day of the year, providing immediate medical care for life-threatening conditions and urgent health concerns. We are equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies with speed, skill, and compassion.

With a dedicated team of emergency physicians, nurses, and support staff, we provide rapid assessment, stabilization, and treatment for patients requiring urgent medical attention. Our state-of-the-art emergency facility is designed for quick access and efficient patient flow.

Emergency Conditions We Treat

  • Heart Attack and Chest Pain
  • Stroke and Neurological Emergencies
  • Severe Breathing Difficulties
  • Major Trauma and Accidents
  • Severe Bleeding and Injuries
  • Poisoning and Drug Overdose
  • Burns and Electrical Injuries
  • Severe Allergic Reactions
  • High Fever and Convulsions
  • Severe Abdominal Pain
  • Fractures and Dislocations
  • All Medical Emergencies

Our Emergency Capabilities

Our emergency department is fully equipped with advanced medical technology including cardiac monitors, defibrillators, ventilators, and point-of-care laboratory testing. We can perform emergency X-rays, ultrasounds, and ECGs on-site for rapid diagnosis.

Trauma Care

Our trauma team is trained to handle severe injuries from road accidents, falls, burns, and other traumatic events. We provide immediate stabilization, surgical intervention when needed, and comprehensive trauma management following international protocols.

We have a dedicated trauma bay equipped with all necessary equipment for resuscitation and life-saving procedures. Our team coordinates with our operation theatre and intensive care units for seamless patient care.

Our Emergency Facilities

Cardiac Emergency Care

Time is critical in heart emergencies. Our emergency department is equipped to provide immediate cardiac care including ECG monitoring, cardiac enzyme testing, and emergency cardiac interventions. We work closely with our cardiology team for optimal outcomes.

Our protocols ensure that patients with suspected heart attacks receive door-to-treatment within the golden hour, significantly improving survival rates and recovery outcomes.

Pediatric Emergencies

Children require specialized emergency care. Our team is trained in pediatric emergency medicine, handling everything from high fevers and breathing problems to accidents and injuries in children. We maintain a child-friendly environment to reduce anxiety during treatment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, you'll be quickly assessed by our triage nurse who will prioritize care based on the severity of your condition. Life-threatening cases receive immediate attention. We keep families informed throughout the treatment process and ensure patient comfort.

After stabilization, patients may be admitted to the hospital, referred to specialists, or discharged with follow-up instructions. Our goal is to provide the highest quality emergency care with compassion and efficiency.
